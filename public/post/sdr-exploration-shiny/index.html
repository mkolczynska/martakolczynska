Shiny App for exploring harmonized survey data (Survey Data Recycling v.1.0)

15 Jul 2018, 13:32
surveys / SDR / WDI / education / R / shiny / plotly

My previous post was about downloading and exploring the Survey Data Recycling (SDR), version 1.0 dataset, which consists of selected harmonized variables from 22 survey projects, 1966-2013.

The SDR project will develop a website for browsing, subsetting, downloading, and visualizing data from the SDR project. This website is currently under construction. Meanwhile, basic functionalities of the future on-line browsing and subsetting tool are available here: https://mkolczynska.shinyapps.io/sdr_data_browser/
